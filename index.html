About

Premier data & AI
consultancy

Nyio Corp is a Canadian enterprise consultancy specializing in the architecture and delivery of intelligent data platforms where cloud infrastructure, real-time data and AI are unified from the ground up.

We engineer platforms with native intelligence rather than bolting AI onto existing systems. Every solution is designed for scale, velocity and adaptability from day one so your organization moves fast without accumulating technical debt.

From large enterprises to high growth ventures, we embed with leadership teams to deliver outcomes that matter: faster time to insight, operational automation and data products that compound in value over time.
